Welcome

My name is Holly. I’ve been reading on and off for 2 years. After the tarot group I was a part of disbanded, I felt a certain loss for the opportunity to practice socially, apart from rare occasions. I’m trying to solidify my spiritual practice and turn it into a consistent part of my life as I’m starting to discover it’s importance to my personal health and wellbeing. I started out with the Steampunk deck by Barbara Moore and Aly Fell. It’s still my personal favorite and the one I’m most experienced with, but I will read with others if requested.

My decks in order of experience:

  • Steampunk, Barbara Moore & Aly Fell (diy, empowering, internally focused with emphasis on forging one’s own destiny and the power of personal choice)
  • AscendedMasters Oracle, Doreen Virtue (positive, uplifting, inspirational)
  • Astrological Oracle, Lunaea Weatherstone & Antonella Castelli (spiritual, subtle, energy based)
  • Thoth, Aleister Crowley & Lady Frieda Harris (deeply symbolic, masterfully crafted)
  • Rider-Waite, Arthur Edward Waite & Pamela Colman Smith (simple, bright, traditional)
  • Gothic, Joseph Vargo (dark, spooky, haunting)
  • Wild Unknown, Kim Krans (minimalist, visually striking)
